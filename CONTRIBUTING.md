# Contributing to Threatpedia

> This document is a placeholder. Contribution guidelines will be formalized during the Founding Board phase (Phase 1, Q2 2026).

---

## How to Get Involved Now

Threatpedia is currently in its founding phase (Phase 0). We are not yet accepting open article submissions or code contributions, but we are actively assembling the founding team.

**Register your interest at [threatpedia.wiki](https://threatpedia.wiki)**

We are looking for:

- **Peer Reviewers / SMEs** — domain experts in ICS/OT, Nation-State, Ransomware, Financial, Supply Chain, and other verticals
- **Cyber Historians / Researchers** — individuals who can validate timelines and narrative accuracy
- **Journalists / Reporters** — professionals who can assess source quality and editorial standards
- **Contributors / Authors** — writers with relevant expertise who want to document cyber history
- **Data / API Users** — researchers and organizations interested in programmatic access to Threatpedia data

Board membership is by invitation only.

---

## What's Coming

When contribution guidelines are formalized, they will cover:

- Article submission standards and required data fields
- Source citation requirements (minimum 3 independent corroborating sources)
- Peer review process and certification criteria
- Framework mapping methodology
- Style guide for article prose and attribution language
- Code contribution guidelines (once the platform is built)
- Talk page standards for disputed attribution and contested facts
